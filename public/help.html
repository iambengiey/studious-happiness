Help Center

Answers to common user questions across login, dashboards, messaging, reports, and school administration.

Home Login

Getting Started FAQs

How do I log in?

Use your school-provided email/username and password on the login page. If you do not have access, contact your school administrator.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Use the password reset option on the login screen. If you still cannot access your account, ask your school admin to resend an invite/reset.

Can I use the portal on mobile?

Yes. The portal is responsive and works on current mobile browsers.

Which roles can access the system?

Platform Admin, School Owner, School Admin, and School Viewer roles are supported, with different permissions.

Dashboard & Data FAQs

Why can’t I see some dashboard data?

Your role or school access may limit what you can view. Ensure you are logged into the correct school tenant.

How often is dashboard data updated?

Data refresh timing depends on your school’s import/update schedule.

Can I export reports?

Yes, users with report access can export supported reports from the reports or dashboard area.

Can I edit attendance/grades/student records?

Only users with edit permissions (for example school admins/owners) can modify school data.

Messaging FAQs

How do I send a school message?

Open the messaging section, choose audience/channel, write your message, and send. Message actions may be role-restricted.

Why can’t I send messages?

Your role may be view-only. Ask your school owner/admin if you need messaging access.

Can I see sent-message history?

Yes, where enabled. Message events are recorded for operational auditability.

School Admin & Owner FAQs

How do I invite a new user?

School owners can invite users and assign allowed roles (such as school admin or school viewer).

Who can change roles?

Role management is restricted to privileged roles such as school owners (and platform admins, where applicable).

Who can transfer school ownership?

Ownership transfer is restricted to authorized owners/admin-level governance flows.

Can a school admin delete a school?

No. School admins can manage school operations but cannot delete or re-own the school.

Access & Security FAQs

How is school data separated?

All school operations are tenant-scoped so users only access data for their assigned school.

Are admin actions audited?

Yes. Key events (invites, role updates, imports/edits, and messaging actions) are logged.

I think I can see another school’s data. What should I do?

Report it immediately to support/your platform administrator with screenshots and time of occurrence.

Troubleshooting FAQs

The page is blank or not loading.

Refresh, clear cache, try another browser, and confirm your internet connection.

I see a 404 page for a link.

The page may have moved or the URL may be incorrect. Return home and navigate from there.

Who do I contact for support?

Please contact your school administrator or your platform support contact.
